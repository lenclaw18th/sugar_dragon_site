Why practices use Sugar Dragon

The gap between your advice and their bathroom

You spend around 15 minutes a year with each patient family. Everything between visits depends on what parents do at home. That gap is where decay happens.

Sugar Dragon gives families a tool — a book, an app, and a brushing chart — that reinforces the clinical message you already give, in a format their child will actually engage with.

23.7% of five-year-olds in England have tooth decay (OHID, 2022). Every copy you stock is a gap closed.
