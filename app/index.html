Two minutes. One dragon. Zero arguments.

The Sugar Dragon app turns tooth brushing into a tiny twice-daily victory. Children brush to defeat the Dragon; grown-ups get a calmer routine and a simple view of progress.

Designed for young children and their grown-ups. No adverts. No child accounts.

The problem it solves

Brushing is simple. Getting a small child to do it rarely is.

For many families, morning and bedtime brushing becomes a negotiation: too tired, too busy, toothpaste tastes funny, two minutes feels forever.

Sugar Dragon gives the routine a story. The toothbrush becomes the child’s tool, the timer becomes the challenge, and finishing the full two minutes becomes a win they can see.

It supports the grown-up without putting pressure, guilt or streak anxiety on the child.

The brushing loop

A routine small enough to use every day.

The app keeps the experience deliberately focused. No noisy menus, no endless rewards, no reason to stay on the screen once brushing is done.

1

Tap Brush

One clear button starts the two-minute brushing session. Morning and evening are shown separately, so the routine is easy to understand.

2

Defeat the Dragon

The Sugar Dragon reacts as the timer runs. Finish the full session and he loses the battle—along with those troublesome wings.

3

Mark the victory

A tooth sticker lands on the calendar. Grown-ups can see recent brushing without turning the experience into a high-pressure scorecard.

Inside the app

Who it is for

Child-first by design

Private. Gentle. Deliberately boring to advertisers.

  • No adverts or third-party tracking
  • No child account required

  • No punishment for a missed session or broken streak
